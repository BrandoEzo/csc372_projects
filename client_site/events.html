Weekly Events

URI Rhody Rumble Weekly

The University of Rhode Island Gaming Club's premier weekly Super Smash Bros and Rivals of Aether 2 tournament!

  • Held in the URI Memorial Union, 50 Lower College Rd, Kingston, RI, 02881, USA
  • Room 312A / 360
  • Every Friday Night during the school year (Sept-Dec) / (Feb-May)
    • Ultimate Singles Starts at 6 PM
    • Rivals of Aether 2 Starts at 6:30 PM
  • 64 Entrant Cap

Weekly Game Night

A new event hosted by Gaming Club to appeal to a more casual audience. Designated times for people to come together and try out some new games they haven't played before! Our entire video game and board game selection is availible to be played during these events!

  • Held in the URI Memorial Union, 50 Lower College Rd, Kingston, RI, 02881, USA
  • Room 312A
  • Every Thursday Night during the school year (Sept-Dec) / (Feb-May)

Special Events

URI Rhody Ruckus Monthly

The University of Rhode Island Gaming Club's (slighly inconsistent) monthly event, featuring Super Smash Bros Ultimate singles and doubles, Rivals of Aether 2, as well as other fighting game side brackets such as Street Fighter 6, Tekken 8, Guilty Gear: Strive, and more!

  • Held in the URI Memorial Union, 50 Lower College Rd, Kingston, RI, 02881, USA
  • Atrium 1/2 or Rainville Ballroom
  • Once a Month Large Scale Events, held on Saturdays
    • Smash Ultimate Doubles at 12 PM
    • Rivals of Aether 2 Singles at 2 PM
    • Ultimate Singles at 4pm
    • Rivals of Aether 2 Doubles at 5pm
    • Fighting Game Times to be Announced
  • 96 Entrant Cap

Rhody Fest

The URI Gaming Club is featured at URI's Rhody Fest, a welcome event designed for Freshman and new URI students to get to know the clubs and activities availible on campus. We bring out a handful of video game consoles and typically run a challenge where if you can beat one of our Super Smash Bros players you win a $5 Dunkin Donuts Gift Card! We bring out some casual games too such as Mario Kart and Mario Party.

  • Held outside the URI Memorial Union, 50 Lower College Rd, Kingston, RI, 02881, USA
  • Held at the start of the school year, typically early September

Sugar Rush

The URI Gaming Club takes part in Sugar Rush, the annual Student Affairs event that takes place around the holidays This event features several clubs and activities, with the Gaming Club bringing out a few different game consoles like Nintendo Switch and Nintendo Wii for students to play while they enjoy copious amounts of free sugary snacks and drinks. A movie is normally played during this event, as well as music by the URI Radio.

  • Held in the Ram's Den of the URI Memorial Union, 50 Lower College Rd, Kingston, RI, 02881, USA
  • End of the Fall Semester, late December
